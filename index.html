✨ Key Features

📋 Sales Tracking

Record all sales transactions with customer details, items, quantities, rates, and automatic GST calculations.

📦 Purchase Management

Track all purchases from suppliers with complete details. Maintain supplier information and transaction history.

📄 Professional Bills

Generate professional invoices with your company details, customer information, and GST breakdown.

💹 GST Tracking

Automatic GST calculation on all transactions. Track GST collected and paid separately.

📊 Financial Reports

Yearly and monthly summaries. View sales, purchases, profit/loss, and GST information at a glance.

💾 Data Backup

Export your data as JSON or CSV. Import data to restore from backups. Never lose your data!

📱 Mobile Friendly

Works perfectly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Responsive design for easy access anywhere.

🔒 Offline Ready

All data stored locally on your device. Works without internet. Your data is completely private.

🎯 How to Use

  1. Set Up: Go to Settings and enter your company name, GST number, address, phone, and email.
  2. Record Sales: In Sales page, add each sale transaction with customer details and amounts.
  3. Record Purchases: In Purchases page, track all purchases from suppliers.
  4. Create Bills: In Bills page, generate professional invoices from your sales.
  5. View Reports: Check yearly and monthly summaries to understand your business performance.
  6. Backup Data: Regular export your data (JSON or CSV) to keep it safe.

❓ FAQ

Is my data safe?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. You have complete control and privacy.

Do I need internet?

No! The app works completely offline. You don't need internet connection to use it.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes! Export your data from one device and import it on another. You can use JSON export/import feature.

What if I clear browser cache?

Your data will be deleted. That's why regular backups (Settings → Export) are important!

Can I customize GST rates?

Yes! Each transaction can have different GST rates. Specify the rate for each sale/purchase.

Is there a cost?

No! Ambika is completely free for all users. Use it for any business purpose without any restrictions.
